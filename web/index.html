Why it's different

Dictation that doesn't phone home

Most voice tools stream your microphone to a server. Voxly runs the speech model on your Mac, so the only thing that leaves your machine is nothing at all.

On-device Whisper runs locally through Metal. Works on a plane, in a café, with the Wi-Fi off. 0 bytes uploaded
Fast From the moment you release the key to text on screen — including the cleanup pass. ~1 second
Anywhere Slack, Mail, your editor, a form in the browser. If you can type in it, you can talk to it. Any app
Yours Free and open source. Bring your own AI for the cleanup step, or skip it and get the raw transcript. No account
How it works

Three steps, then it's muscle memory

Hold

Press and hold the right ⌘ key. A small indicator tells you Voxly is listening.

Speak

Say it how you'd say it out loud. Trail off, correct yourself, change your mind — that's the point.

Let go

The finished text appears wherever your cursor was. Nothing to copy, nothing to paste.

Modes

Same voice, different output

Switch modes from the menu bar. Each one rewrites what you said for a different purpose.
