How it works

Three steps, then it's muscle memory

Hold

Press and hold the right ⌘ key. A small indicator tells you Voxly is listening.

Speak

Say it how you'd say it out loud. Trail off, correct yourself, change your mind — that's the point.

Let go

The finished text appears wherever your cursor was. Nothing to copy, nothing to paste.

Modes

Same voice, different output

Switch modes from the menu bar. Each one rewrites what you said for a different purpose.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is it really free?

Yes. No account, no trial, no subscription. You bring your own AI for text cleanup — a local Ollama or an API key — or use none at all: raw transcription works out of the box.

Which Macs does it run on?

Apple Silicon (M1 or later) on macOS 13+. Intel Macs aren't supported.

Where does my voice go?

Nowhere. Audio is transcribed on your Mac by a local Whisper model. If you connect a cloud AI for cleanup, only the text is sent — never audio.

Do I need Ollama or an API key?

No. Without any AI, Voxly pastes the raw transcription — already well punctuated. With one, it also tidies grammar and formatting according to the mode you're in.

Does it work offline?

Yes — transcription is fully local. Only optional cloud cleanup and update checks need a connection.

What languages does it support?

Whisper large-v3-turbo understands ~99 languages, strongest in English and Spanish. Voxly follows your system language automatically.

How do updates work?

The app checks a version file at launch and shows "Update available" in the menu bar. Download the new DMG, replace the app, done.
